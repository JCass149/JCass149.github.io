About Me

I’m an innovative and enthusiastic Computer Scientist who studied at the University of Durham from 2015-2019.

Born in Stockton-On-Tees, I moved to Durham to study in the field of computing, after discovering a genuine desire to better understand how technology works, and how it can be used for countless amazing things, from medical research to improving quality of life through efficiency and inter-connectivity.

Throughout my time studying, I’ve learned how the technology we use everyday functions, networked with some extremely talented individuals, and competed at levels of sport I never thought I’d be able to.
