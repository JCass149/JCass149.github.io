About Me

I’m a young, innovative and enthusiastic third year Computer Scientist studying at the University of Durham.

Born in Stockton-On-Tees, I moved to Durham to study in the field of computing, after discovering a genuine desire to better understand how technology works, and how it can be used for countless amazing things, from medical research to improving quality of life through efficiency and inter-connectivity.

Throughout my time studying so far, I’ve learnt so many mind-blowing things about how the technology we use every-day functions, met some extremely talented and amazing individuals, and competed at levels of sport I never once thought I’d be able to and can’t wait to see where the next few years will take me.
